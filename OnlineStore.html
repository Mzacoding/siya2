About Us

Black-Owned Organization GL Consulting Established in 2023

GL is a Young-Black-Woman-Owned Engineering company at Level 1 B-BBEE certified company.

We are a varied provider of industrial supply, waste management, cleaning, engineering, maintenance and construction services.

We offer consulting services and engineering project management from inception phase to final project handover and we make exceptional efforts to optimize on cost effective solutions.
